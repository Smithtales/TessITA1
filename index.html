Pet Health Web App

Overview & Motivation

Lots of people have pets, their health is important as valuable members of many families. With our busy lifestyles it is easy to let the less vocal members of our families health waver. While human health is an area that has many options for monitoring and tracking our own health, the health of our pets is not usually documented or tracked with the same frequency, granularity or accuracy. For our pets, this data is not regularly updated or collated. Without a system to make it easy to collect and share this information, vets also may not have the most up to date information. The software packages they use for storing or sharing this information on patient history make it difficult to share updates and care advice with their customers about their patient. I propose developing an app that consolidates all of this information at the press of a button, that will collate all the care information on your pet, such as when they are due for checkups or when they have had their last vaccination.

Description

To solve this problem, I propose developing a web-app that collects information supplied by the vet on each visit and stores it in an online database. The web app could also be paired with a smartphone app that provides the same functionality but in an easily accessible format. The database will store the following information about each animal:

  • pets name
  • owner information
  • breed
  • age
  • gender
  • history
  • treatment history
  • billing information
  • medication
  • home care management plans
  • important dates and reminders
  • contact information
  • microchip data
  • vaccination information and schedule
  • weight
  • activity levels
  • general demeanor
  • clinical notes
  • owner notes and observations
  • issues and incidents
The system will allow both the owner and the vet to be able to view, update and share the information in real-time to ensure the best care for the pet. Both vets and owners will be able to access and update the database via a web interface, or via the smartphone app. Once access is granted, users will be able to interact 24 hours a day, 7 days a week with the database to ensure current information and alerts are able to be viewed as soon as it's needed. The app can be linked to include payment options such as afterpay, payment agreements with the clinic, or credit cards to help pet owners manage the ongoing expenses of pet ownership and veterinary care. Information in the system is also analysed to identify when urgent care alerts should be issued, and when appointments are coming up or have been missed. Threats are identified to the pet's health or wellbeing may include:
  • upcoming or missed vaccination appointments
  • changes in weight or activity levels
  • test results that require follow up or consultation
  • health alerts based on location and animal breed (such as viral outbreaks such as cat flu or myxomatosis)
The app will have different profiles for each animal, which would be especially beneficial for people with multiple pets. Owners could view individual profiles and reports detailing what each animal was treated for, what their ongoing health is expected to be and when they need a follow up visit. Owners could also access a breakdown of the expenses past, present, and future to help people navigate the day to day expenses of pet ownership. Vets could prescribe medications and other care management activities such as weight and activity monitoring that the owner can perform at home. Once submitted buy the owner, this information is made available in real time to the vets and for analysis, reporting and alerts.

Tools & Technologies

The following technologies are needed to build the solution:

  • The solution will require a database that can be accessed via a web page.
  • To access the web page, users will need to register an account and be issued credentials to be able to log into the system.
  • The web server and database will be hosted in the cloud (e.g. AWS, Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud Platform)
  • A smartphone app capable of connecting to and providing up-to-the-minute data also needs to be developed to ensure ease of use.
